WHAT WE DO

A description of us

The National Security Agency is an agency focused around collecting information about foreign groups. Anything from Cartel de Noreste to the Russian Federation. We collect information and keep that information very secure. The NSA is widely regarded as the department with the best case files, having copius amounts of information on every major gang, cartel, militia, and more.

How we do it

The NSA collects information through many forms of surveillance, spying, and cyberwarfare. Details on technologies that we use can be found in the info tab.
