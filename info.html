ANT Specializations

Offensive - Has full access to all of NSA's offensive tools. The main goal of this person is to attack a target, whether to gain information or damage their infrastructure. Will take logs from a Defensive Agent or utilize offensive tools to find a vulnerability in a target machine.

Defensive - Has full access to all of NSA's defensive tools. The main goal of this person is to repel any attack against NSA's security systems and/or servers. It is also this person's goal to make sure that none of NSA's technology gets into the wrong hands. After a defensive action is taken and systems are secured, they will log and send over information about the attacker to the Offensive Agent.

Sentinel - This is a Defensive or Offensive Agent that has access to one of NSA's Sentinels. Sentinels are armored vehicles used to defend the premesis of NSA's headquarters. Every Sentinel has a 'Recollection Protocol' and unless hit with a heavy weapon, will be able to diagnose and repair the damage.
