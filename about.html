ABOUT US

Our Mission

The National Security Agency is a national-level intelligence agency of the United States Department of Justice, under the authority of the Director of National Intelligence. The NSA is responsible for global monitoring, collection, and processing of information and data for foreign and domestic intelligence and counterintelligence purposes, specializing in a discipline known as signals intelligence (SIGINT)

Locations

The NSA has locations and offices all across the state of California, with a main headquarters at postal 9393 on the right side of the map.

Partnering

The NSA partners with multiple departments to provide high-quality case files and to train their agents to be self-sufficient in collecting and storing information. The NSA currently assists the Unites States Marshals with case files in a joint task force code-named 'Cipher'.
